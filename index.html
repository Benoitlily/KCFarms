KC Farms, a reputable poultry farm in Ghana, takes pride in producing premium Hala chickens that meet the highest standards of quality and taste. With a commitment to excellence and a focus on animal welfare, KC Farms ensures that their chickens are raised in a safe and healthy environment, resulting in tender and juicy meat that is free of antibiotics and hormones.

Live poultry

Our live poultry, is a carefully curated collection that features healthy, vibrant birds raised in optimal conditions, ensuring superior taste and nutritional value. 

Fresh Eggs

Our fresh eggs, harvested daily from our meticulously tended flocks. Our commitment to quality starts from the ground up, with our hens enjoying spacious and comfortable living conditions, coupled with a balanced diet rich in nutrients.

Chicks
